My Foray into
Geographic
Information
Science

 Back at Missour State University, when I was studying City Planning, I took a GIS class. One thing led to another, and I found myself In the GIS department persuing a GIS Certificate. In one of my GIS classes, I was instructed to build a website to host my GIS projects on. That website no longer exists, but the projects live here now,
 Only the "Tiny House Villages for the Homeless" project was something I completed for a salary, the rest are just my old class exercises that I couldn't find it in me to leave behind on my now defunct website.
  As a side note, I would absolutly love to make a return to the GIS-o-sphere; I really enjoyed my time as a cartographer.
